## Note on Rankings

This list was compiled from multiple sources including:
- TopWebComics.com voting rankings
- WebcomicsHub ratings
- Web search results from 2025-2026
- Historical popularity data
- Award winners and nominees
- Traffic and subscription data from platforms

The rankings reflect a combination of current popularity, historical significance, cultural impact, and consistent readership. Some webcomics have completed their runs but remain highly influential and widely read.
